Common Questions

What certification levels are covered?
All NREMT certification levels from EMR through Paramedic. Our question bank covers every topic area on the NREMT exam.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees. You keep access through the end of your billing period.
Do I need my program to sign up for me?
No. Students can subscribe individually. If your program has institutional access, ask your instructor for enrollment details.
What is CAPCE?
The Commission on Accreditation for Pre-Hospital Continuing Education — the national standard for EMS continuing education quality. Our CE courses are CAPCE-accredited.
How does the adaptive learning engine work?
Our system tracks your performance on every question and identifies weak areas. It then prioritizes those topics in your study sessions so you spend time where it actually matters — not re-studying what you already know.
What does the institutional plan include?
Full platform access for every student, a free administrative account, and program-level analytics and reporting. Instructors get exam building, clinical tracking, lab tracking, and gradebook tools at no extra cost.
Is my data secure?
Yes. We take student data privacy seriously. Contact us for details about our data handling practices and compliance.
How is MedEdPrep different from other NREMT prep tools?
Most test prep tools give you a question bank and call it a day. We combine adaptive learning with clinical tracking, program analytics, and instructor tools — and we're the only platform contracted by the Georgia Trauma Commission for competency evaluations.
