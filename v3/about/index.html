It Started With Better Questions

Jeramey Ricks was an EMS educator who couldn't find questions that challenged his students the right way. So he wrote them. Then he built a platform around them. Then programs started calling.

Then State Agencies Took Notice

MedEdPrep was contracted by the State of Georgia to lead remediation efforts with OEMST, delivering documented successful outcomes. The Georgia Trauma Commission adopted our testing software for competency evaluations — serving over 1,000 students annually with a 20% increase in pass rates.

Now We're Building the Team to Scale

Heather Reddick joined as Director of Development. She spent years as a Georgia state official overseeing EMS program designation — she knows what evaluators look for because she used to be one. Our EOPA exams are accepted by the Georgia Board of Education as one of three approved exams for high school EMS programs. And we offer free test prep to any high school program in the nation.
